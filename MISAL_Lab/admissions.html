Admissions

Course-wise admission information for research and postgraduate programmes at MISAL Lab, IIT Patna.

Before You Apply

Review the programme details, identify your research interest, and go through recent work from the lab so your application and interview are aligned with the department's focus areas.

Ph.D. Admission

Full-time Research Programme

The Ph.D. admission track is intended for candidates who want to pursue advanced research in microwave engineering, RF systems, integrated circuits, and related domains.

Applicants should monitor the institute admission advertisement for application opening dates, fee details, required documents, and programme-specific instructions.

  • Application is submitted through the official institute portal.
  • Shortlisting is based on academic background, eligibility, and department criteria.
  • Relevant background in electronics, electrical engineering, communication, or allied areas is expected.

Shortlisted candidates may be called for a written test and/or interview. The evaluation usually focuses on conceptual clarity, analytical thinking, and readiness for research work.

  • Core fundamentals from the candidate's discipline.
  • Problem-solving approach and ability to reason from first principles.
  • Alignment of research interests with MISAL Lab themes.

Applicants should revise subject fundamentals, read about ongoing research themes, and prepare a clear explanation of why they want to work in the selected area.

Helpful preparation areas: microwave fundamentals, RF circuits, antennas, signals and systems, communication basics, and prior project work.

MS(Research) Admission

Research-Oriented Master's Programme

MS(Research) is suitable for students who want a strong research foundation with a structured postgraduate pathway and thesis-focused work.

The MS(Research) admission cycle follows the institute notification. Candidates should check the advertisement for programme-specific eligibility, application requirements, and selection stages.

  • Applications are invited through the institute admission portal.
  • Shortlisting may include academic record and relevant technical preparation.
  • A good fit with lab research areas strengthens the application.

The selection process may include an interview or technical interaction to evaluate subject readiness, research aptitude, and suitability for guided research work.

  • Questions may cover circuits, communication, devices, and mathematical basics.
  • Interviewers may discuss previous projects, internships, or dissertation work.
  • Candidates should be able to explain why they prefer MS(Research) over coursework-heavy options.

Focus on technical basics, research motivation, and clarity around the kind of thesis work you want to pursue.

Good preparation includes: revising core concepts, organizing project summaries, and reading about present microwave and RF research challenges.

M.Tech Admission

2-Year Postgraduate Programme

M.Tech admission is aimed at candidates looking for advanced technical training with a blend of coursework, laboratory practice, and project-based specialization in RF and microwave systems.

M.Tech admissions are conducted through the institute process for postgraduate programmes. Candidates should follow the admission advertisement for category-wise seats, eligibility, and timelines.

  • Application details, deadlines, and required documents are announced by IIT Patna.
  • Eligibility is based on qualifying degree requirements and institute norms.
  • Preference may be given to candidates with strong fundamentals in electronics and communication systems.

Selection may include merit-based shortlisting and departmental evaluation as per the current institute policy.

  • GATE score and/or institute criteria may be used for shortlisting.
  • Some cycles may include interview or additional interaction.
  • Final selection follows programme seat availability and published norms.

Candidates should strengthen core ECE concepts and keep project/internship work ready for discussion.

Useful focus areas: network theory, signals and systems, analog and digital circuits, communication systems, electromagnetics, and microwave engineering basics.

Important Notes

Final admission dates, eligibility conditions, and fee details should always be taken from the official IIT Patna admission notice.

Candidates should prepare a concise statement of purpose aligned with microwave, RF, or integrated system research.

Interview panels often value conceptual understanding more than memorized answers.

Relevant coursework, project reports, internships, and publications should be organized before the interview stage.
