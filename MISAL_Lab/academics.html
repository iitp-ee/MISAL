Ph.D. Program

4-5 Years | Full-time Research

Our doctoral program offers advanced research opportunities in cutting-edge microwave technology, RF circuit design, and integrated systems. Candidates conduct independent research under faculty guidance to contribute novel solutions to the field.

The Ph.D. programme is a 60-credit research degree programme, typically spread over 4-5 semesters for a full-time student. Focus is on conducting original research and writing a dissertation that reports these results.

Key Features:

  • Advanced research facilities and state-of-the-art laboratories
  • One-on-one mentorship from leading faculty members
  • Opportunities for publications in top-tier international journals
  • Industry collaboration and internship opportunities
  • Stipends available for GATE-qualified candidates

MS(Research) Program

2 Years | Research-Focused

Most Popular

A research-focused Master's program that combines theoretical knowledge with practical research experience. Students undertake significant research projects in microwave systems, signal processing, and circuit optimization.

The MS(Research) programme is a 60-credit degree programme spread over 2 years for full-time students. About two-thirds of credits involve coursework, and one-third consists of project work focusing on original research.

Key Features:

  • Research-oriented curriculum with thesis component
  • Flexible specialization options
  • Industry internship opportunities
  • Seminars and workshops with leading researchers
  • Financial support for GATE-qualified students

Q: What is the difference between MS(Research) and M.Tech?

A: MS(Research) is more research-oriented with emphasis on original contributions, while M.Tech has more coursework and industry focus.

Q: Can I convert to Ph.D. after MS(Research)?

A: Yes, excellent performers can convert to Ph.D. program mid-way.

M.Tech Program

2 Years | Industry-Focused

An industry-relevant Master's program that focuses on practical applications of microwave engineering. Students gain expertise through coursework, projects, and internships with leading technology companies.

The M.Tech programme is a 60-credit degree programme spread over 4 semesters for full-time students. About two-thirds of credits involve coursework, and the remainder consists of project work. The program emphasizes practical skills and industry readiness.

Key Features:

  • Industry-focused curriculum and practical training
  • Mandatory internship with leading companies
  • Hands-on projects and laboratory work
  • Career development and placement support
  • Collaboration with industry partners
