Metasurfaces & Metamaterials: Reconfigurable surfaces aur wave manipulation

Theme Current Direction Methods and Tools
Metasurfaces & Metamaterials Designing reconfigurable intelligent surfaces (RIS) for wave-front engineering and polarization control. Ansys HFSS, CST Studio Suite, Unit cell analysis, Periodic boundary conditions.

OAM Antenna Systems: Orbital Angular Momentum for high-capacity wireless

Theme Current Direction Methods and Tools
OAM Antenna Systems Generation of high-order Orbital Angular Momentum modes using phase-plate and circular array configurations. Full-wave EM simulation, Phase-shifting networks, Spiral phase distribution analysis.

Reflectarrays & Transmitarrays: Beamsteering aur high-gain reflector designs

Theme Current Direction Methods and Tools
Reflectarrays & Transmitarrays Development of wideband, high-gain antenna arrays for long-range beamsteering and satellite links. MATLAB-based synthesis, Array factor optimization, Unit-cell phase characterization.

RF & Microwave Circuits: Filters, power amplifiers, aur passive components.

Theme Current Direction Methods and Tools
RF & Microwave Circuits Designing high-rejection filters and efficient power amplifiers for sub-6 GHz and mmWave bands. Keysight ADS (Advanced Design System), Vector Network Analyzer (VNA), SIW technology.

MIMO & 5G/6G Antennas: Sub-6 GHz aur mmWave antenna arrays

Theme Current Direction Methods and Tools
MIMO & 5G/6G Antennas Optimization of massive MIMO arrays with focus on mutual coupling reduction and high isolation. Decoupling networks, Diversity gain metrics, Multi-port S-parameter analysis.
